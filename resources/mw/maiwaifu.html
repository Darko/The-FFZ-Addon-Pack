Nakoruru (ナコルル) is a character in the Samurai Shodown series of fighting games. Her character is based upon the Ainu people of Hokkaido, Japan. Nakoruru's role in the game is of a defender of Mother Nature; in some material Nakoruru has been referred to as a priestess.
